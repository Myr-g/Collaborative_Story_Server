action|A fast‑paced narrative emphasizing physical conflict, daring feats, and dynamic movement.
adventure|A story driven by exploration, risk, and bold journeys into the unknown.
comedy|A humorous story focused on wit, situational humor, or comedic character interactions.
coming-of-age|A story centered on personal growth, identity, and the transition from youth to adulthood.
drama|A character‑focused narrative emphasizing emotional conflict, tension, and personal stakes.
dystopian|A story set in an oppressive or degraded society, exploring themes of control, survival, and resistance.
fairytale|A whimsical or magical story rooted in classic fairytale tropes and moral lessons.
fantasy|A collaborative fantasy story. Maintain magic consistency and world lore.
folklore|A tale inspired by traditional myths, legends, or cultural storytelling motifs.
gothic|A dark, atmospheric story featuring decay, mystery, and emotional or supernatural tension.
historical|A narrative grounded in a specific historical period with attention to accuracy and context.
horror|A tense horror story focused on atmosphere and psychological dread.
lovecraftian|A cosmic‑horror narrative emphasizing the unknowable, the incomprehensible, and human insignificance.
mystery|A mystery story where clues must be logically consistent.
nonsense|A playful, absurd story that embraces illogical events and whimsical language.
paranormal|A story involving ghosts, spirits, or unexplained phenomena that blur the line between natural and supernatural.
philosophical|A reflective narrative exploring abstract ideas, ethics, or the nature of existence.
romance|A character-driven romance focusing on emotional development.
satire|A story that uses humor, irony, or exaggeration to critique society, politics, or human behavior.
sci-fi|A science fiction narrative grounded in speculative technology.
slice-of-life|A grounded, everyday narrative highlighting ordinary moments, subtle emotions, and personal reflection.
thriller|A high‑stakes, suspenseful story driven by tension, danger, and escalating stakes.
tragicomedy|A narrative blending humorous elements with serious or tragic themes.
